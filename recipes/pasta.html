My Authentically Italian Pasta Recipe

Description

Get a taste of authentically Italian pasta! With rich sauce that's extremely easy to make, this will make a weekday dinner that much better.

Ingredients

  • Canned peeled, whole tomatoes
  • Parmesano Reggiano cheese
  • Pasta of your choice
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Half a large yellow onion/medium sized yellow onion

Cooking Instructions

  1. Blend a can of whole tomatoes
  2. Thinly slice 3-4 cloves of garlic
  3. Thinly dice half of a large yellow onion, or a whole medium sized yellow onion
  4. Coat the bottom of a pan with olive oil
  5. Heat pan on medium-high heat
  6. Put in onions and sautee until translucent
  7. Put in garlic and sautee for 30-45 seconds
  8. Pour in blended tomatoes
  9. Boil pasta until your preferred doneness
  10. Drain pasta and serve with sauce and grated parm.
